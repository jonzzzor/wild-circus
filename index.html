About Us

Welcome to Wild Circus

Wild Circus is a small, non-human, brick-made and for profit organization based in Billund, Denmark. Since 1923 Wild Circus has been bringing the most oustanding tranformation of Circus Art to people : a circus made of bricks.

Kids love it, parents love it, older folks loves it, even kitties love to see our shows !

Due to our duplicable DNA (which is basicly plastic) Wild Circus can perform all around the world, at as many places we can and that at the same time !

The Brick's family tradition

Since generations, our billions family-members are working hard to entertain humans.

Come and see Houdibrick, the only magician that car pull the Great Brickwall of China from his hat !

Brickwise the Dancing Clown, will fool around with the audience and make you laugh so loud that you will cry.

At last but not the least, enjoy Felindra of Fort Brick making her tigers battle in an epic Puss in Boots sad eyes contest !
